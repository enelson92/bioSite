All About Lori...

Lori Lucente-Nelson is an award-winning real estate agent in Orlando, FL. As a member of both the RE/MAX Executive Club and the 100 Club, Lori’s main goal is to help home buyers get the house of their dreams. As a real estate agent, Lori works with buyers looking to find their forever home, as well as with sellers who are looking to relocate to their forever home. Lori has lived in Orlando, FL for 20 years, which makes her an invaluable resource in locating and selecting neighborhoods and homes for her clients.

Prior to her successful real estate career, Lori was an educational professional in several spheres. She ran one of the top Gymboree Play Program franchises for children based out of Dearborn Heights, Michigan. Additionally, she was a veteran school teacher for some of the private and charter schools in the West Orlando area.

When she is not working and breaking real estate records, Lori enjoys traveling and spending time with her family and friends. She enjoys creating new memories with her family and children, whether that is going on a Disney Cruise Line vacation, or staying at home watching movies with freshly made air-popped popcorn. One of her greatest passions is her children. She is the mother to six beautiful children, two of which are adopted (one daughter is from Guatemala, and one daughter is from China). Lori currently resides in Orlando, FL with her family and Australian Shepherd, Zoey.
